The proposed timeline is as follows\footnote{Note that for the 21/22 academic year, announcing categories will occur in the May staff meeting}:

For the academic year X/Y:

\begin{description}
    \item[\textbf{Start of September, X}:] Announce categories at the staff meeting for new academic year
    \item[\textbf{Through June, Y}:] Do general advertising
    \item[\textbf{Mid June, Y}:] Start the vote
    \item[\textbf{End of June, Y}:] Ask teachers to post reminders for voting
    \item[\textbf{End of June}:] Collect the vote
    \item[\textbf{Start of July, Y}:] Announce winners at the staff meeting
    \item[\textbf{Start of July, Y}:] Conduct interviews
    \item[\textbf{Mid July, Y}:] Announce winners to the general public, and release interviews
\end{description}
